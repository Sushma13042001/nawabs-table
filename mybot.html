Meet NawabBot

NawabBot is an AI chatbot trained on Nawab's Table complete menu. Ask about any dish — its ingredients, spice level, price, or whether it's vegetarian.

Try asking NawabBot:

"What biryanis do you have?" "Is Haleem vegetarian?" "Which dish is least spicy?" "How much does Chicken 65 cost?" "Tell me about Mirchi Ka Salan" "What desserts are available?" "Recommend something for vegetarians"

📝 Team Reflection

NawabBot was built to let customers ask natural questions about Hyderabadi dishes — like "which biryani is best?" or "what's vegetarian?" — without having to scroll through the full menu. We loaded the 10-dish dataset into a Botpress Table, connected it to a Knowledge Base, and wrote Autonomous Node instructions telling the bot: "You are NawabBot, a friendly food assistant for Nawab's Table. Only answer questions about our Hyderabadi menu using the knowledge base." The biggest challenge was keeping the bot on-topic — it kept recommending things outside our dataset until we tightened the instructions. Through this project we learned how structured data, AI language models, and web deployment connect together to create something genuinely useful for real restaurant customers.
